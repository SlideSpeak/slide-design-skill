Where the next 5 points of margin will come from

Channel mix
Shift 18% of unit volume to owned channel by FY28; recover 6-9 points of channel margin while building first-party data assets.
Product portfolio
Concentrate launches behind three hero SKUs that index highest in DTC pilot regions; sunset 14 long-tail SKUs underperforming in wholesale.
Operating model
Stand up a 22-person DTC squad (marketing, ops, data, CX) reporting directly to CEO for first 18 months to maintain decision velocity.

The 36-month entry plan, in five phases

Step 01
Stand up
Months 1-6. Recruit DTC lead, ship MVP storefront, launch in two regional markets with three hero SKUs.
Step 02
Validate
Months 7-12. Reach $2M run-rate, prove CAC < $42, build subscription waitlist.
Step 03
Scale
Months 13-22. National launch, expand to 8 SKUs, scale paid acquisition with $4.2M media budget.
Step 04
Diversify
Months 23-30. Add quiz-led discovery, launch subscription, integrate with Northwind retail loyalty program.
Step 05
Optimize
Months 31-36. Margin push: own-brand packaging, regional fulfillment, predictive replenishment.

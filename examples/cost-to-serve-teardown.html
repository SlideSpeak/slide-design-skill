Where the cost per parcel goes

Cost to serve has outrun price, and the gap is bounded, not structural.

Meridian serves a parcel for EUR 4.18 against a EUR 3.40 peer benchmark. The 23% premium is concentrated in four operational lines, every one of which is addressable without touching price.
4.18EUR / parcel
Cost base EUR 294M, FY27 management accounts. Benchmark is the median of four regional peers, normalised for drop density. Illustrative.3

Which levers close it, and in what order

Four levers walk the EUR 4.18 down to the EUR 3.40 benchmark.

Cost-per-parcel bridge to benchmarkEUR per parcel
Worst-run lever: Returns handling
How to read the bridge
Line-haul consolidation is the largest step at EUR 0.31, trunking volume onto fewer, fuller runs
Depot automation removes EUR 0.22 of manual sortation labour per parcel
Route density takes EUR 0.19 as stops per route rise toward peer levels
Returns handling is only EUR 0.06 but the worst-run lever per unit, and the quickest to fix
1. Deltas are per-parcel run-rate at full ramp
2. Returns marked worst-run by cost-per-event, not by total euros

Which levers close it, and in what order

Eight initiatives carry the cost-out, each with an owner and a sized prize.

Lever, owner and sized prizeRun-rate, EUR M / year
Line-haul consolidation trunks volume onto fewer, fuller runs. Network ops. EUR 12.6M / yr
Dynamic dispatch re-optimises routes through the day. Field engineering. EUR 11.4M / yr
Route-density redesign lifts stops per route toward peers. Network planning. EUR 7.8M / yr
Depot automation removes manual sortation labour. Depot operations. EUR 6.2M / yr
Returns triage clears the worst-run lever first. Customer operations. EUR 2.1M / yr
Driver scheduling matches shift cover to parcel curves. Workforce planning. EUR 1.9M / yr
Failed-delivery reduction cuts redelivery attempts. Customer operations. EUR 1.4M / yr
Packaging right-sizing lowers cube and line-haul fill cost. Procurement. EUR 0.9M / yr
1. Run-rate at full ramp, not first-year
2. Owners are functional leads accountable for delivery
